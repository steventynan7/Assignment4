Obtaining My Private Pilot License

Requirements Overview

Becoming a private pilot is a straightforward process, but it takes a lot of time and determination. Below are some of the requirements:

  • Be at least 17 years of age
  • Be able to read, speak, write, and understand English
  • Hold a U.S. student pilot certificate
  • Hold a third-class medical certificate
  • Meet aeronautical experience requirements
  • Meet aeronautical experience requirements
  • Pass the knowledge test
  • Pass the practical test

Benefits of Obtaining a Private Pilot License

  • Act as pilot in command of an aircraft
  • Carry passengers
  • Fly for charitable events
